Productivity Tips

  • Use the Pomodoro technique during study blocks (25 min focus, 5 min break)
  • Schedule the most challenging tasks during your peak energy hours
  • Use free time for physical movement to counter long study sessions
  • Ensure study environment is well-lit and free from distractions
  • Combine similar tasks to reduce context switching
  • Review and adjust schedule weekly based on what's working
